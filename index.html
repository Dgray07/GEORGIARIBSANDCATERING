Our Story

More Than BBQ.
It's Family.

Georgia Ribs & Catering was born from a love of real Southern cooking — the kind that takes all day and fills the whole neighborhood with smoke. We're a family operation with a big custom smoker and even bigger heart.

Every dish on our menu comes from scratch-made recipes passed down through generations. Our ribs are rubbed, smoked, and sauced in-house. Our sides — mac & cheese, green beans, baked beans, cole slaw — are cooked fresh every day.

Whether you're pulling up to our food truck or booking us to feed your entire event, you're getting the same thing: the sweet taste of the South.
